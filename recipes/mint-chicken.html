Mint Chicken

Description

This delicious recipe combines chicken breasts with lemon juice and mint leaves to create a family favorite! Serve over cooked white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves sliced into bite-size pieces
  • 1 crushed glove garlic
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. In a medium sized bowl, mix together the chicken pieces and garlic. Toss in flour just to coat the meal and shake off the excess.
  2. Heat the margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces; cook and stir until browned. Add the mint leaves and lemon juice, cover and stream for about 5 minutes, just until the chicken is cooked through.
